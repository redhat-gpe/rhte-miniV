This workshop is intended to give you a hands on introduction to using OpenShift from the perspective of a developer. For developers looking to kickstart their projects, OpenShift enables efficient application development through streamlined workflows and validated integrations.

A sample lab guide can be found here:
https://openshift-labs.github.io/starter-guides-html/

There are 4 programming language variants of the workshop:

  • Java
  • Node.js
  • Python
  • .NET

When provisioning completes, look for the email that you get from RHPDS, and take note of the Homeroom URL. Lab content is under labs project, then share homeroom Route URL with your attendees. When accessed, they will need to provide a username and password. Available usernames correspond to the number of users requested when you ordered the environment:

Usernames: user1 .. userN
Password: openshift

If a user totally hoses their environment, they can log out and then choose a different user by clicking the menu in the upper right corner of the Homeroom interface. They can also clear cookies.

Provisioning time: ~75 min
